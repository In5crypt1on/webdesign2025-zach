What is a AAA Game?

The term "AAA" (pronounced "Triple-A") is an informal classification used within the video game industry to signify games produced and marketed by large, major publishers.

These titles are characterized by three primary factors:

  • Massive Budgets: Development and marketing costs often exceed tens or even hundreds of millions of dollars.
  • High Production Values: Expect state-of-the-art graphics, cinematic scores, full voice acting, and complex physics engines.
  • Large Development Teams: Teams frequently comprise hundreds of developers, artists, and engineers, spanning multiple years of production.
